## Metric direction

Most important metric is **human interaction** (corrections per hour). Alongside:
overall WER, and domain-specific WER (τοπωνύμια, νομικοί όροι). See
[finetuning-101 → what to measure](../reference/finetuning-101.md#what-to-measure).

## Midterm goals (July 10th)

- Dataset review tool.
- A dataset ready and published on Hugging Face.
  - Rough target: ~25k utterances / ~30 h audio — ~20% from the tool, ~30%
    random corrections from the full dataset, ~50% uncorrected human-reviewed.
  - 20% of 25k = 5k utterances; ~15 s to review one → ~4–5 h per person for 5
    people.
  - Split into train / val / test — **split per subject/meeting, not per
    utterance** (see [decisions/data.md](../decisions/data.md#2026-06---split-trainvaltest-by-whole-meeting-not-by-utterance)).
- Measure baseline performance.
- Select 1–3 models to fine-tune.
